About MKULIMA Seedlings Ltd

Founded in 2015, MKULIMA Seedlings Ltd has grown to become one of Kenya's leading suppliers of certified seedlings. We are committed to supporting Kenyan farmers with high-quality, disease-free planting materials.

Our nursery in Nairobi produces over 500,000 seedlings annually, serving farmers from Mombasa to Kisumu. We specialize in fruit trees, vegetable seedlings, forestry trees, and ornamental plants.

  • KDB & KEPHIS Certified Nursery
  • Tissue Culture & Grafted Varieties
  • Over 10,000 Farmers Served
  • Free Agronomic Support
